Who We Are

About AranChez

Born in Cebu in 2026, AranChez was founded on a simple belief: great haircare shouldn't cost a fortune. We craft affordable, high-quality products for every Filipino's hair journey.

🌿 Our Story
Our Mission

Healthy Hair for Every Filipino

We started with a simple question: why is quality haircare so expensive? AranChez was built to answer that — with effective, nature-inspired formulas made accessible to all.

From our first shampoo to our growing line of serums and treatments, every product is crafted with care, tested for safety, and made to deliver real results.
